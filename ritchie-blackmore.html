Ritchie Blackmore (Guitarist) - On This Day

Profession: Guitarist

Biography: Ritchie Blackmore is an English guitarist and songwriter, known as a founding member of the band Deep Purple. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame with the band in 2016, and is commonly regarded as one of the most influential guitarists in the history of rock music.

Born: April 14, 1945
Birthplace: Weston-super-Mare, England
Age: 79 years old

Generation: Silent Generation
