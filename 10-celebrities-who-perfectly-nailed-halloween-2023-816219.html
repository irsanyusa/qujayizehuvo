10+ Celebrities Who Perfectly Nailed Halloween 2023

As the fall leaves change colors and the air grows cooler, Halloween excitement is in the air. Celebrities have been getting into the spirit, with some paying tribute to their friends and colleagues and others putting a unique twist on classic characters. Get ready for some Halloween fun as we explore the top celebrity costumes of 2023.

Adele as Morticia Addams

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Baker with their “Beetlejuice” costumes.

Cindy Crawford as Sandy from “Grease”

Keke Palmer as the Bride of Frankenstein

Paris Hilton as Britney Spears from the hit “Toxic”

Paris Hilton had a second costume this year, cosplaying Katy Perry’s iconic mushroom look.

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly as The Bride vs Gogo Yubari

Halle Bailey and her boyfriend as characters from “Poetic Justice”

Robert Griffin as Harry Potter

Justin and Hailey Bieber as characters from “The Flintstones”

Demi Lovato as Snow White

Bonus: Kourtney Kardashian recreated one of Kim Kardashian’s most iconic looks for Halloween, Kim’s first Met Gala outfit.

Stuck on the hunt for your ideal Halloween costume? Look no further — these ideas might just be the inspiration you need to make your choice!
