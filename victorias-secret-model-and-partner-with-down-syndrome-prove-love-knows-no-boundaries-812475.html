Victorias Secret Model and Partner With Down Syndrome Prove Love Knows No Boundaries

Sofía Jirau is not just a model, she’s a breath taker. Her striking beauty and infectious personality have shattered stereotypes and proven that anything is possible. Born with Down syndrome, Sofía was told by many that she would never achieve her dreams. But she refused to be defined by her diagnosis and instead chose to pursue her passion for modeling. And now, the lovely model has found a boyfriend.

Sofía Jirau and her boyfriend, Chris González, show us what a cute couple looks like.

Despite having grown up with Down syndrome, González and Jirau are showing the world what they’re made of. González is an entrepreneur, while Jirau is a stunning model, and both of them are on a mission to prove that there are no limits to what they can achieve. Their dedication and hard work have paid off, and they’ve already made history in their fields by setting some seriously smart goals.

The fabulous Sofía Jirau became the first woman with Down syndrome to be featured in a Victoria’s Secret Love Cloud collection campaign. At just 25 years old, she’s already making waves in the modeling industry and inspiring others to follow their dreams. In fact, since she started modeling in 2020, she’s been a shining example of how perseverance and hard work can help you overcome any obstacle.

The lovely couple is breaking all barriers.

“I want to tell the whole world that people with my condition, Down syndrome, inside and out, we have no limits,” she explained. “People who have Down syndrome like me are capable of getting a job, creating their own business, and working hard like me.”

And let’s not forget about Chris González, the other half of this amazing power couple. In 2014, he made history by becoming the first person with Down syndrome in Puerto Rico to obtain a driver’s license (according to his parents). But that’s not all — he’s also a skilled chef, a talented barista, and a successful entrepreneur with his very own food truck. With so many incredible accomplishments under his belt, it’s clear that González is a true trailblazer who refuses to let anything hold him back.

And the power couple also runs a campaign.

Chris González and Sofía Jirau are teaming up to make a difference and inspire others through their incredible campaign called “Sin Límites” (or “Without Limits” in English). This dynamic duo is on a mission to raise awareness about the Down syndrome community and to demonstrate that anything is possible with hard work and determination.

People recognize Jirau on the street and often stop to take pictures with her because of her growing fame. And when talking about her busy schedule, the lovely model explained that, to keep up with her busy lifestyle, she hits the gym for an hour 5 days a week.

Their relationship has been described in the past as an example of love at first sight. Although Jirau and González have kept quiet about their relationship since early 2022, there is no indication that they have split up or plan to do so anytime soon.
