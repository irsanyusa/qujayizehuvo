The Masked Singer has released a new performance from Season 10, featuring contestant Gazelle. Get ready to be wowed, as Gazelle just cemented themselves as a frontrunner in this season’s competition. Fans are already speculating about their identity.

Gazelle Performs on The Masked Singer Season 10

In the newly released performance, Gazelle takes the stage to sing “Uninvited” by Alanis Morissette. The new contestant has an elaborate costume, with long horns and a robe covered in flowers. They also have an incredible voice.

The panelists are clearly impressed by Gazelle’s powerhouse performance, and fans in the YouTube comments are already predicting that they’ll win the entire competition. People are also guessing about Gazelle’s identity.

One popular guess about who might be under the mask is Broadway star Idina Menzel. Other guesses include Belinda Carlisle, Ariana DeBose, and Carrie Underwood. Whoever it is, they’re obviously incredibly talented.

During Sunday’s Season 10 kickoff episode, Gazelle introduced themselves with a clue about their identity. They teased that Season 4 contestant Gremlin (Mickey Rourke) was somehow connected to them.

Season 10 Premieres September 27

The Season 10 competition officially kicks off on Wednesday, September 27, on FOX. The show has already introduced us to several new contestants, including Donut, Hawk, S’More, Hibiscus, Diver, Royal Hen, Husky, Cow, Anteater, and Tiki.

Panelists Ken Jeong, Jenny McCarthy, Nicole Scherzinger, and Robin Thicke are all returning to the show for their 10th season together. Nick Cannon is also returning as the host.

The first unmasking of the season took place during Sunday night’s kickoff episode, as special guest Anonymouse performed “What About Love” by Heart and was later revealed to be singer Demi Lovato. Anonymouse isn’t part of the official Season 10 competition.
