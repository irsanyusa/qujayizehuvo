Social Media Feud Erupts Between 'RuPaul's Drag Race' Queens Luxx Noir London, Trinity The Tuck

Reading Time: 2 minutes

RuPaul’s Drag Race queens Luxx Noir London and Trinity the Tuck are currently in a social media feud that has been taking place on Twitter. This particular disagreement has fans talking and has even been mentioned on Reddit.

Trinity the Tuck is Targeting Luxx Noir London Over Season Favorite Picks

The conflict began when Trinity, who is known for being a winner on RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars season four, tweeted that she wanted Anetra, Sasha, and Mistress to win Drag Race. She did not mention Luxx, who is a front-runner in the competition. After some fans pointed out that Trinity had not mentioned Luxx, Trinity responded by saying that she simply had her favorites and that she loved all three queens equally.

Luxx took to Twitter to express her feelings about the situation. She tweeted that she would be “rotted” too if she spent as much money as Trinity to look like her. Luxx also accused Trinity of throwing shade at her and supporting negative comments about her on social media. She then brought up allegations that Trinity was behind a shady Reddit account.

Trinity responded to Luxx’s tweet by quoting it and saying that she had not said anything about Luxx. Trinity then stated that she did not like Luxx and that Luxx had a “nasty attitude.” Luxx clapped back, asking Trinity to stop throwing “sideways shots” at her.

Fans have been divided over the feud, with some supporting Luxx and others supporting Trinity. Some fans pointed out that Luxx has been critical of other queens on the show, and has invalidated their drag. Others have criticized Trinity for not being more supportive of Luxx, who is a fellow drag queen.

Fans Call For Unity Amidst Trinity’s Feud With Luxx

While the two queens have been at odds for a while now, their recent social media exchanges have left fans divided, with some taking Luxx’s side and others supporting Trinity. But amidst all the drama, many fans are calling for unity and reminding everyone that the show is ultimately about celebrating the art of drag.

The drag community has been known to have a history of disagreements and rifts. It has been suggested that it is crucial to set aside these petty differences and concentrate on the larger objectives.

In contrast, some fans believe that Drag Race has played a significant role in bringing the drag community into the public’s attention. As a result, it is vital to support every participant, regardless of personal preferences or views.
