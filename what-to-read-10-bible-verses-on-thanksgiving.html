10 Bible verses on Thanksgiving

Being thankful for little things or our big things like our existence is an act of showing appreciation, Below are Bible verses that would teach you one or two about thanksgiving.

1. Psalm 95: 2-3

ADVERTISEMENT

Let us come before him with thanksgiving and extol him with music and song. For the LORD is the great God, the great King above all gods.

2. 1 Corinthians 1: 4-5

I always thank my God for you because of his grace given you in Christ Jesus. For in him you have been enriched in every way—with all kinds of speech and with all knowledge

3. Psalm 107:22

And let them offer sacrifices of thanksgiving, and tell of his deeds in songs of joy!

ADVERTISEMENT

4. 1 Thessalonian 5:18

Give thanks in all circumstances; for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus for you.

5. 1 Timothy 4:4

For everything created by God is good, and nothing is to be rejected if it is received with thanksgiving,

ADVERTISEMENT

6. Philippians 4:6

Do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God.

7. Amos 4:5

Offer a sacrifice of thanksgiving of that which is leavened, and proclaim freewill offerings, publish them; for so you love to do, O people of Israel!” declares the Lord GOD.

8. Jonah 2:9

ADVERTISEMENT

But I with the voice of thanksgiving will sacrifice to you; what I have vowed I will pay. Salvation belongs to the LORD!

9. Jeremiah 30:19

Out of them shall come songs of thanksgiving, and the voices of those who celebrate. I will multiply them, and they shall not be few; I will make them honored, and they shall not be small.

10. Psalm 69:30

I will praise the name of God with a song; I will magnify him with thanksgiving.
