Kelly Ripa Posts Flirty Comments On Vacation Photos Of Husband Mark Consuelos

Just this week, Kelly posted a series of photos, showing off her husband Mark on her Instagram page while they were on a ski trip together. The first photo Kelly posted was one of Mark carrying skis decorated with the Mexican flag. #Equipomexico , she captioned a photo.

It's in the comments section, that things started to heat up. One fan wrote "#daddy," a nickname that the Live With Kelly and Ryan host has used before to describe her hubby of 23 years . And Kelly wrote back playfully, forever #daddy.
