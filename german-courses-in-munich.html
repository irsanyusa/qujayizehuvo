How to learn German in Munich

German students in Munich have quite a few options. Local colleges and universities often provide classes for foreign students resident in the city. Summer camps are a good way to prepare for the fall semester. Specialist language schools run in-person courses from central locations. And you can always hire a private tutor at a slightly higher cost. But for cost-effective and flexible learning, online courses like Lingoda are the best way to go.
