Education

In 2004, she enrolled at the University of Birmingham to study psychology. She received a Bachelor of Science degree from it in 2007 (1st class with honours).

Following that, she pursued a Master's Degree in psychology at the University of London. She received her Master's Degree in Organizational Psychology from it in 2008.

Two years later, she enrolled at the University of Leeds, where she earned a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Organizational Psychology and Behaviour Change. In 2018, Phillipa earned a Practitioner Diploma in Executive Coaching and Certification in Systemic Team Coaching.

Career

Philippa was studying and working as a psychologist at the same time. She was a visiting lecturer at the University of Birmingham's Institute of Occupational and Environmental Medicine from 2009 to 2010.

She worked as an Associate Consultant Psychologist at Work Psychology Group from 2009 to 2012. In 2012, she began working as a Business Psychologist for Independent Consultancy Work.

In 2015, she founded the organization STRIDE and became a Business Psychologist & Environmental Behaviour Change Specialist. In 2019, Coan began providing coaching and training for Stride Coaching.

She now contributes to the teams’ work, focusing her efforts on maximizing their collective abilities to meet performance goals and specified objectives. Psychologist Phillipa Coan believes that this collaboration is the only effective way to achieve long-term sustainability and resource efficiency.
