Do cockroaches bite and spread diseases?

Sep 7, 2024 Besides being unsettling to look at, are they a real threat to our health? Do cockroaches bite? This is a good question, and the answer is... not really! While cockroaches have mouths with tiny jaws, they aren't like mosquitoes that pierce your skin to suck blood. Their bites are more like scratches from their spiny legs. These scratches are unlikely to break the skin and cause any serious harm.

Un prix total de l'eau en hausse en 2022
