Best Cyber Monday Deals in 2023, From TVs to Clothing Sales

If you decided to skip Black Friday this holiday season but have a change of heart after the weekend is over, don’t fret. Cyber Monday is here to help you with online offerings of steals and deals on many of the same product categories—clothes, tech, televisions, appliances, beauty, and computers are the staples here—without the potential requirement of having to shop in-store (and change out of your pajamas).

The term “Cyber Monday” was coined in 2005 by the National Retail Federation. However, Black Friday and Cyber Monday are two sides of the same commerce-related coin, with brick-and-mortar stores (historically) holding sales starting at midnight on Black Friday and online retailers offering up reduced prices on Monday. Nowadays, Black Friday weekend into Monday (and in some cases much earlier)represents days of deals for shoppers.

When is Cyber Monday?

Cyber Monday is the e-commerce equivalent of Black Friday. It is always the Monday after Thanksgiving, the fourth Thursday in November. In 2023, this means Cyber Monday will occur on November 27, 2023.

What deals should I expect on Cyber Monday?

Similar to Black Friday deals, Cyber Monday sales usually include discounts on tech, fashion, appliances, home goods, and travel. Online furniture stores often run deals, and, of course, given it's the holiday season, toys and kid-related merchandise are popular items. In addition, many online retailers offer free shipping during Cyber Monday sales. You can also find deals on popular items like the best air fryers and mattresses.

What kind of Cyber Monday deals were last year?

Last year, you could find markdowns on goods from major online retailers, including Amazon, Wayfair, Walmart, and others. Brick-and-mortar stores, like J.Crew and The Gap, offered special Cyber Monday deals separate from their Black Friday sales.

How to get a good deal on Cyber Monday

If you have skipped Black Friday completely and are starting your holiday shopping on Cyber Monday, don’t panic. There are so many sales out there. Maybe too many, haha, if you are easily overwhelmed.

Even shopping from the comfort of your home or office can be overwhelming on Cyber Monday because there are so many deals. Knowing what you need or want (grab a pen and make a list ahead of time) and where to check for sale prices on these items will help.

Setting up Google alerts, following your favorite brands on social media, and signing up for store emails - remember to check your spam box- are all ways to track the best offerings for Cyber Monday.

How to choose the best Cyber Monday deals for you

Decide what to buy (ahead of time)

Shopping online can lead to overspending, so decide what you want to buy ahead of time to help keep your priorities in order. For example, if you are looking for Black Friday tech deals or Black Friday travel deals, you might find them now advertised on Cyber Monday with further markdowns.

Comparison shop

Drop your loyalty to one brand over another when shopping on Cyber Monday. Check and double-check sellers for the items you want and see who offers the best markdowns.

Make a budget

Overspending during the holidays can lead to a very unhappy new year for your finances. Create a budget ahead of time and address what you can afford to spend on yourself and others during the holidays - especially during large sale events where it is easy to scoop up deals on things you might not need.
