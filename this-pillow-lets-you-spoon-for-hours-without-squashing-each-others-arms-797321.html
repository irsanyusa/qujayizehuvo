This Pillow Lets You Spoon for Hours Without Squashing Each Others Arms

Falling asleep cuddling with your beloved one is the sweetest of all pleasures, but it can end in a split second when your arm gets numb. The Coodle Pillow company team knows how we all love spooning, and they’ve designed a pressure relief pillow that will let you sleep “on” your partner’s arm without making it numb.

Here at Bright Side, we believe that cuddling pillows are the new thing, and here’s how they work.

Coodle Pillow, or cuddle pillow, as its designers call it, is made in the form of an arch that is meant to protect the arm of your partner from the weight of your head. Internal braces make the pillow rigid, so it resists the pressure well, and doesn’t let you or your partner squash each other’s arms while cuddling.

One of the best things about the Coodle Pillow is that you can easily slip out of it, like if your partner falls asleep on your arm, without bothering them. You can make yourself some tea or a bunch of popcorn and then come back to your “little spoon” like you never went away. The pillow is made of high-quality foam and you can take it with you when traveling.

The Coodle Pillow is available in 2 sizes — standard and large, and it may also come in handy if you like to sleep on your own arm, or it can just be a good support for your neck.

You can buy this magical pillow on the Coodle Pillow official website or on Amazon.
