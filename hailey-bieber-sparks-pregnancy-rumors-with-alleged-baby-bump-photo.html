Hailey Bieber Sparks Pregnancy Rumors with Alleged Baby Bump Photo

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for Roc Nation

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Supermodel Hailey Bieber has once again sparked pregnancy rumors. Apparently, fans believe that she and Justin Bieber are expecting their first child after seeing a baby bump photo of the socialite.

Fans Think Hailey Bieber is Pregnant

A new photo of the married couple is circulating online, giving rise to fans’ speculation about Hailey’s pregnancy. In the stolen snap, the model can be seen enjoying Drake’s concert backstage alongside her husband.

Justin was sitting atop a giant speaker while wearing a white T-shirt, denim shorts, and a black hat. Hailey was also wearing a hat, sporting a pair of jeans and sleeveless black crop top.

The supermodel wife held her hands crossed over her exposed belly, which fans believed to have exposed a baby bump. Apparently, several fans have noticed the seemingly visible bump, thus asking if Hailey is currently “pregger.”

“It does look like she’s pregnant,” one user wrote.

“Her hands on her bellyyyy!” another fan wrote.

Additionally, on TikTok, user @thekylemarisa stated that the couple was allegedly not drinking alcohol when the photo was taken. Furthermore, the same user claimed that Hailey consulted with a fertility specialist back in December 2022 and has successfully gotten pregnant after she and Justin tried to make one.

Her Vogue Japan Photoshoot Fueled the Rumors

Most recently, the 26-year-old model landed on the cover of Vogue Japan. Sharing some of her snaps on Instagram, fans happened to point out a sudden change in her fashion style.

Some photos showed Hailey in layered and baggy outfits, further supporting the public’s pregnancy theory. In the photos, Hailey was looking glamorous as she wore a loose black dress and a large sweater.

Having uploaded these photos, fans believe that the supermodel is dropping hints about her first child. However, neither Hailey nor Justin confirmed nor denied the allegations.

The star couple has been married for almost five years now. They officially tied the knot during a September 2018 New York City courthouse ceremony. A year later, they got married in front of friends and family in a more formal wedding.
