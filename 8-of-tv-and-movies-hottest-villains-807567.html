8 of TV and Movies Hottest Villains

When it comes to movies and TV shows, we all root for the heroes, hoping for a happily ever after. However, we can’t help but fall for some hot baddies from our favorite films. No matter how many dangerous acts they commit or how bad they are, something is drawing us to these hot villains, and all we can do is feel that conflicting love/hate for them.

Being bad has never looked so good, so today at Bright Side we’ve gathered the 8 hottest TV and movie baddies of all time.

1. Tyler Durden, Fight Club

Fight Club is one of the most well-known movies from the 1990s. Tyler Durden, the narrator’s alter ego, is played by Brad Pitt, and that’s all we need to know about him to consider him the hot bad guy. Tyler is ferocious and extravagant, and while he may not be a woman’s ideal man, his dark gaze, coupled with his iconic red jacket, has won the hearts of many women.

2. Maleficent, Maleficent

Angelina Jolie is not only the hot villain in Maleficent, but she is also one of Hollywood’s hottest actresses. In this Disney film, she plays Maleficent, the malevolent fairy, and she is portrayed in the darkest, most charming light possible. Those cheekbones, her long black clothing, light eyes, and bold red lips are the distinguishing features that make this character even more appealing.

3. Loki, Marvel Cinematic Universe

Throughout the Marvel Saga, there have been many hot baddies, but only a few have been as well-liked, and have been able to pique the audience’s interest, as Loki. One of the most prominent antagonists in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, played by the stunning Tom Hiddleston, has won many hearts with his charming and charismatic appearance, which is appropriate for the most heinous villains in movies.

4. Harley Quinn, DC Extended Universe

Margot Robbie gave her all in her portrayal of Harley Quinn in the Suicide Squad, and she undoubtedly created an iconic and hot villain. Her red lips with her fuchsia-blue ponytails are her distinguishing features. Margot Robbie’s depiction of Harley is so convincing that her Halloween costume has surpassed the traditional nurse and witch costumes.

5. Draco Malfoy, Harry Potter (Film Series)

In the Harry Potter franchise, there have been many hot bad guys worth admiring, like Tom Riddle’s handsome teenage version of Voldemort, but none as foxy as Draco Malfoy, played by Tom Felton. Despite his bad-boy behavior, many series fans adore Draco’s blonde hair and green eyes, which give him the appearance of a fairy tale prince, which he definitely is not.

6. Poison Ivy, Batman & Robin

Uma Thurman is not only a magnificent and versatile actress, but she is also a dazzling woman who played the popular and hot movie character, Poison Ivy, in Batman & Robin. With a comb of her venomous kiss and lethal temperament, the appealing Poison Ivy has undoubtedly spread her poison to bring all men to their knees.

7. Damon Salvatore, The Vampire Diaries

In real life, coming across a vampire would terrify us, but some vampires aren’t all that horrible in the end, and Damon Salvatore in The Vampire Diaries, starring Ian Somerhalder, is unquestionably our favorite. Piercings and a dismal haze, combined with biting sarcasm, make him a hot baddy, and we can’t blame Elena Gilbert for falling for someone like him.

8. Madison Lee, Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle

Demi Moore is attractive in any role she takes on, but playing the hot villain is a natural fit for her. In Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle, Demi plays Madison Lee, a former angel who is also a formidable opponent. Demi looks fantastic in the film, with her jaw-dropping physique emerging from the waves.

Deeply affected by her mother Marcheline’s passing, Angelina Jolie penned a touching eulogy, highlighting their close bond. Marcheline’s sacrifices and dedication as a single mother deeply influenced Jolie’s own parenting style, emphasizing love and compassion.
