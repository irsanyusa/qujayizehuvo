15 People Share Cool Stories That Almost Seem Unbelievable

Most of us have that one story that sounds too made up to be real and no one believes it, but it’s actually true, like accidentally cold-calling a friend who you haven’t spoken to in a decade. Don’t worry, we believe every word you say.

Here at Bright Side, we are believers. That’s why we’ve put together a list of 15 stories for you to enjoy.

1.

During my second year, I lost my coat however it wasn’t just a case of me leaving it somewhere. It turned out that it had somehow been pulled up into the sky and while I was telling the teacher that I had lost my coat and it landed on my friends head. He then came into the classroom to tell me and was very surprised.

3.

I once checked in at a restaurant and gave the hostess my name to hold my slot. She then asked for my last name since the person below shared my first name. When I told her my last name, she looked at me like I was lying. The person below had my first and last name. She laughed and introduced us to each other, and we took a pic. I’ve never met someone with the same first and last name as mine.
© ghperry / Reddit

5.

I was 9 at a playground near a school. I saw a giant Christmas tree with presents through the school glass doors. I took a brick and threw it through the window, grabbed as many presents as I could, and then ran out. The presents were just props.
© Warm_Enthusiasm_9852 / Reddit

7.

I had a pet hummingbird as a kid. My mom found it injured on a sidewalk and nursed it back to health. It only had one eye and no depth perception so we kept it inside. We’d let it buzz around the room, land on us, and all that jazz. My brother told his teacher about it and the teacher actually called my mom and was like “your son has made up a crazy story and insists it’s real”. The reality was we had a pet hummingbird and that seemed crazy enough that people didn’t believe it.
© seanzorio / Reddit

9.

11.

I worked with someone for years. We were work friends. Never hung out or talked after work, but I knew him well enough. One day I’m chatting with him in the line at lunch and I ask him about something we both had worked on the day before and he looks at me like I’m insane. Later that day, he comes and asks me about the exact same case. Turns out Brandon had an identical twin that worked for our company in a different department.
© DisownedByMother / Reddit

13.

When I was 5 my mom married my stepdad. After the wedding ceremony, I went to the bathroom. When I came out everyone was gone. Not a car or person in sight. I knocked on the priest’s door. He knew us pretty well and offered to drive me to my mom’s friend, who was the maid of honour and lived close-by. On the way there we passed my mom’s friend who was rushing back to find me. Everyone thought I just drove back with someone else.
© HenRob_6327 / Reddit

15.

In college, I was walking to class and a thunderstorm hit, but I had just gotten a girlfriend and I was in the best mood of my life so I was just getting soaked and smiling from ear to ear. A guy walked by with an umbrella, I smiled at him and said hi. He took down his umbrella and got soaked with me and we proceeded to talk about how moments like these are what make life magical.
© ThinkIGotHacked / Reddit
