Lionel Messi: Christophe Dugarry tenders apology for calling Barcelona star half-autistic kid

- Lionel Messi has gotten an apology from ex France international Dugarry

- Christophe Dugarry made some unpleasant comment towards the Argentine

- The 48-year-old was angry with Griezmann playing only two minutes against Atletico

Barcelona supertar Lionel Messi has been offered an apology by former France international Christophe Dugarry for calling him a half-autistic kid after game against Atletico Madrid.

The Spanish champions were forced to a 2-2 draw in what was a disappointing result for Setien and his wards considering their fight to retain the Spanish La Liga title.

Bur what made Dugarry annoyed during Barcelona's game against Atletico was the introduction of his fellow countryman Antoine Griezmann two minutes before the end of the game.

Antoine Griezmann was unable to play to his own satisfaction considering the limited time he had on the pitch as the encounter ended in a draw.

While speaking on RMC Sport, Dugarry claimed that Antoine Griezmann lost his confidence in the game against his former side.
