As you may already know Mr. Pants is an America’s Got Talent comedian that tells dad jokes about pants. There’s more than what meets the eye with this comedian, here’s everything you need to know about him.

Ryan Dee, known on America’s Got Talent under his stage name, Mr. Pants, is a comedian that hails from New York City. It appears that Ryan has suited up as Mr. Pants since the beginning of 2020. Even though he’s suited up as Mr. Pants since 2020, his comedy career dates back until at least 2017. This comedian currently has 1,375 followers on Instagram and 488 followers on TikTok.

At this time, Mr. Pants can be seen cracking pants jokes all over New York City. His social media videos feature him cracking jokes at places like Coney Island, New York City Pride, and more.

Ryan Gee Makes Headlines For His Conveniently Placed Poop Emoji Statue

Outside of comedy, Ryan has made the news all over New York City, but not for his zany persona. In 2021, Ryan was in the news for another joke. At the time, he was attempting to get a permit for a smiling poop sculpture to place behind the iconic Wall Street Bull. Ryan’s poop emoji statue was bronze colored, featuring a giant smile. Reportedly, Ryan had been placing the sculpture behind the Wall Street Bull discreetly for quite sometime before seeking a permit.

The permit would allow the sculpture to be placed behind the Wall Street Bull once a year. Though it seems like Ryan never got the official permit, the poop sculpture was still quite the joke.

We’re not quite sure how Ryan began acting as Mr. Pants, since he has a convenient pant-related joke in his Instagram bio in place of a website. America’s Got Talent fans cannot wait to see what else Mr. Pants brings to the table in the live shows. Could his simple, yet clever, comedy secure him a spot in the finale?
